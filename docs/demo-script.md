# Demo Script

1. Open `/`.
   Explain that PaperGraph turns papers into a living social research graph.

2. Open `/app`.
   Show the cockpit: thesis, paper feed, memo preview, and graph.

3. Open `/app/feed`.
   Show daily curiosity cards with relevance reasons and social metrics.

4. Save or heart the top paper.
   Explain that these events later drive graph updates and recommendations.

5. Open `/app/papers/shape-descriptors-loao`.
   Show insight cards for contribution, method, and advisor question.

6. Open `/share/post-1`.
   Show a research-native repost with context and actions.

7. Open `/app/graph`.
   Show paper -> descriptor -> thesis and claim -> thesis paths.

8. Open `/app/assistant`.
   Ask: `What should I read next to validate my LOAO thesis?`

9. Open `/app/thesis-validator`.
   Show overlap risk, novelty hypothesis, confidence, and missing evidence.

10. Return to `/app`.
   Close with: Google Scholar shows what has been cited. PaperGraph shows what people are reading, questioning, and building from.
