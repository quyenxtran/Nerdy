# PaperGraph AI MVP Scope

## Product Wedge

The first build should prove one product belief:

Reading papers can become social, visual, cumulative, and useful for thesis validation.

## Primary Demo Loop

1. Open the landing page.
2. Enter the app cockpit.
3. Review a daily paper card with a personal relevance reason.
4. Save or heart the paper.
5. Open the paper detail page and scan insight cards.
6. Repost the paper with a research question.
7. Open the graph and see the paper connected to claims, descriptors, and thesis nodes.
8. Ask the assistant a cited question.
9. Run a thesis validation report.
10. Generate or preview a weekly advisor memo.

## Non-Goals For The First Working Demo

- No full public social network.
- No Google Scholar scraping.
- No production PDF parser.
- No Neo4j.
- No billing.
- No complex recommender training.
- No exhaustive literature-review claims.

## Implementation Standard

Every feature should preserve provenance:

- paper ID or source URL
- source page or chunk when available
- generated card ID
- confidence or uncertainty when AI is used

The UI may feel energetic, but research claims must remain cautious and traceable.
